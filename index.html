Considered design. Mostly for users, sometimes just for fun.

I'm a UX designer with a background in accessibility, branding, and interaction design. I care about making digital things work for everyone — using good structure, inclusive thinking, and a bit of curiosity.

I've designed services for the public sector, charities, and global companies. I enjoy working with developers, mentoring designers, and solving awkward problems.

Outside of work, I'm a Director of CamCreatives CIC — a large and still growing creative community in Cambridgeshire.

I've also run nightclubs, managed record shops, and spent most of my life learning how people tick.

Always creating. Always aware. Always improving.
